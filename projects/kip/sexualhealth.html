Sexual health

Services

56t is a holistic sexual health and wellbeing service for all trans and non-binary people and their partners.

No appointment needed. Wednesdays 4:30pm to 7:00pm, level 2, 56 Dean Street. Of course you are welcome at all other times when the clinic is open.
