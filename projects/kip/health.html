Information regarding physical and general health

Some factsheets have been prepared by members of the National LGB&T Partnership in collaboration with cliniQ, Mermaids, TransForum Manchester,with support from Public Health England.

The Factsheets have the following objectives:

  • To promote the health and wellbeing of trans people
  • To provide trans people with basic information about key aspects of good health and wellbeing and how secure it
  • To signpost sources of information and support

Trans-only and trans-friendly sports and physical activities

London

  • Trans and Non-conforming Swimming (TAGS) hold regular sessions for trans people to swim in a trans-only space.
  • cliniQ run a regular yoga session on Wednesday evenings for all trans and gender variant people.
  • Josetta Yoga also runs a trans and queer-inclusive class in Bethnal Green, East London.
  • Gendered Intelligence have a regular football kick around in Southwark.
  • Irreverent Dance is a LGBT-friendly and body positive dance space in Kentish Town, North London. Classes in a range of dance styles are offered.
