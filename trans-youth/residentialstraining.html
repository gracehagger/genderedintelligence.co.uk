GI Training Course for Trans Residential Workers

This June, in collaboration with The Kite Trust, we will be running an overnight, weekend training course for trans, non-binary and gender diverse people with an interest in youth work. Completion of this course will enable you to volunteer on residentials with GI and with The Kite Trust. For more information and to apply for a place, please download the application pack, here. If you attended the GI training course in 2019, please complete this form instead. **Deadline**: before midnight on 10th May
