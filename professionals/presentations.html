Gendered Intelligence Speakers

Why not book a Gendered Intelligence speaker for your next diversity event?

Gendered Intelligence can provide amazing speakers, who draw on their passion for a more inclusive world to deliver key note speeches and to take part in panel discussions, Q&As and other similar activities. These can be for conferences, equality and diversity events, social networks and other activities.

To book a GI speaker or make an enquiry about costs, email events@genderedintelligence.co.uk.

If you are looking for more formal presentations or facilitated workshops that deliver specific learning outcomes, please visit our training page.
